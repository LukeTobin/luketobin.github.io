House of Influnce

Game

A social deduction game for the Unidice. Deduce who are you allies or foes and figure out others intentions.

The project was created for a new technology called the Unidice. I worked on the game during my time at my internship with ExtraNice.

The game can be viewed here

Project Details

Company: ExtraNice

Year: 2021-2023

Project Form: Commerical

Project Length Personal contribution spanned 6 months

Engine: Unity

Source Control: PlasticSCM

Responsibilities: UI Design, Playtesting, Contributed to Game Design

Notable Work

UI Design

UI mockups and flowcharts were created for the game. Flow charts were developed to break down the gameplay loop into more manageable steps, ensuring a smooth experience for players, and providing guidance for the whole development team. Extensive research was conducted to identify the target audience and design appropriate UI elements. Multiple mockups were then created for each screen, resulting in the final UI design seen in the product today.
