Hopplip

Concept

A puzzle game mixing the simple mechanics of hopping between tiles & "rock, paper, scissors". Made for Weekly Game Jam 154 with the theme of "Rock, Paper, Scissors".

The game was developed within a 2 day schedule.

Gameplay

Water ripple effects
