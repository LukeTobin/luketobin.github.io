Sleep Pet

Concept

A virtual pet mobile game that aims to alleviate habits in individuals struggling with ADHD. The application has been specifically designed to help users improve their sleep patterns and overcome bad habits by utilizing a variety of cutting-edge techniques. This project was a graduation project for a client and went through the typical game development cycle that included research, conceptualization, prototyping, and evaluation phases.

Project Details

Team Size: 1

Project Form: Graduation Project for a Client

Project Length 6 Months

Engine: Unity

Source Control: Plastic SCM

Responsibilities: Development, Design & Research

Notable Work

  • Completed the entire game development cycle, from concept to completion
  • Used extensive research methodologies, including desk research, literature studies, target audience surveys, and analysis of best, good, and bad practices
  • Employed various ideation techniques during the conceptualization phase, including 20 ideas, brainstorming, mindmapping, biomimicry, weighted matrix, and competitive analysis
  • Visited the prototyping stage multiple times to apply various iterations and overhauls based on evaluation results, including mockups and product research
  • Conducted evaluations of the product at multiple stages, using targeted playtesting, post-test surveys, expert reviews, usability tests, think-aloud protocols, and observation to arrive at the final product
