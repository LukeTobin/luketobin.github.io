Penguin Chef

Concept

A fast-paced and chaotic cooking game where you (and potentially a friend!) take on the role of a penguin chef. The objective is to combine and cook various foods by colliding them together, all while trying to fulfill customer delivery requests for specific dishes!

The game was developed within 48 hours for Ludum Dare 53 with the theme "Delivery"

Project Details

Team Size: 2

Project Form: Game Jam

Project Length 2 Days

Engine: Unity

Source Control: Plastic SCM

Responsibilities: Development & Design

Notable Work

  • Implemented modular & robust physics based cooking system
  • Conceptulization around a specific theme
  • Translated a design to invoke desired emotions from the users
  • Worked on a tight schedule
  • Polished and cleaned end product
