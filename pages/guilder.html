Guilder

Tactical Turn Based Roguelite with heavy emphasis on guild building. Recruit and control your group of heroes as you try reach the end. Synergize, and conquer the foes within your path, unlock new heroes and epic artifacts and make every single one of your runs fun and unique.

For Guilder I was the lead and only developer, working on various large systems:

  • Pathfinding
  • Player Level
  • Enemy AI
  • Ability System
  • Item System
  • Unit control
  • Combat
  • Recruiting System
  • Roguelike Experience

Development for this project lasted 16 weeks over spring. We used the scrum development methodology & github for version control.
