Luke Tobin

Hello! I'm Luke, an aspiring technical game designer from Ireland. I'm currently living in The Netherlands, studying Game Design. I have always been interested in games and the fields related to their development.

I have always wanted to join the industry from a young age and help build up experiences for people through creative and diverse ways. I enjoy the puzzle solving elements which come with programming and design tasks, as it allows for me to come up with unique and creative solutions to interesting problems. I love working alongside various disciplines to come together and create interesting, fun and memorable experiences. I'm an incredibly dedicated & flexible designer that can quickly pick up new skills and tasks, adapt & tackle difficult problems.
