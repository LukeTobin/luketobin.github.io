Luke Tobin

Hello! I'm Luke, a 21 year old programmer and designer from Ireland, I'm currently living in The Netherlands studying CMD Game Design. I have been programming for over 7 years now going from making mods for games to creating fully finished products. My main interests when it comes to game development are gameplay programming and scripting. I enjoy the puzzle solving aspect which comes with programming that allow for me to come up with unique and creative solutions to problems. I love working alongside designers to create interesting and fun mechanics. I'm incredibly dedicated & flexible developer and I can quickly pick up new skills or tasks.
