Luke Tobin

Hi! I'm Luke, a passionate and ambitious technical game designer from Ireland. I'm currently living in The Netherlands, after having just finished my bachelors degree in Game Design. With a deep-seated interest in games and all aspects related to their development, I am determined to leave my mark in the industry and create immersive and engaging experiences that captivate players.


My fascination with programming and design tasks lies in the inherent puzzle-solving elements they offer. This challenges me to think outside the box and devise novel and innovative solutions to intricate problems. I relish the opportunity to collaborate with diverse disciplines, leveraging their expertise to create enthralling and unforgettable game experiences. My unwavering dedication, flexibility, and ability to quickly adapt and learn new skills makes me a valuable asset to any team.
