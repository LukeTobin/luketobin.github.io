Luke Tobin

Hi! I'm Luke, a passionate technical game designer from Ireland. I'm currently living in The Netherlands, where I obtained my Bachelor of Science in Game Design & Development and am currently freelancing. With a deep-seated interest in games and all aspects related to their development, I am determined to leave my mark in the industry and create immersive and engaging experiences that captivate players.


My fascination with programming and design tasks lies in the inherent puzzle-solving elements they offer. The opportunity to research & refine standardized and innovative solutions to intricate problems, is something I deeply enjoy. I relish the opportunity to collaborate with diverse disciplines, leveraging their expertise to create enthralling and unforgettable game experiences. My unwavering dedication, flexibility, and ability to quickly adapt and learn new skills makes me a valuable asset to any team.
